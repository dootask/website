# Message Forwarding

In team communication, you often need to share a piece of chat content with others or with a group. **DooTask** supports both single-message forwarding and **merge forwarding**, making it more convenient and organized to share chat content.

## Single-Message Forwarding

1. In the chat, right-click (long-press on mobile) the message you want to share.
2. Select [Forward].
3. Choose the target conversation (member or group) and confirm to send.

## Merge Forwarding

When you need to share multiple messages at once, use **merge forwarding** to bundle multiple messages into a single record and forward them. The recipient can click to view the complete chat context.

1. In the chat, enter **multi-select mode** (long-press a message or enter via the menu).
2. Check the multiple messages you want to share.
3. Select [Merge Forward], then choose the target conversation and confirm.

::: tip Use Cases
- Hand over the full context of a discussion to relevant colleagues;
- Summarize key decisions in a group and forward them to a supervisor or another group;
- Avoid the screen flooding and broken context caused by forwarding messages one by one.
:::
